Contact

Interested in my work or considering comissioning some custom furniture? Send me a message below and I'll get back to you as soon as possible. Or scroll a little bit further to see some frequently asked questions.







FAQ

"I'm interested in [insert piece of furniture]. How much does that cost?"

Cost depends on a number of factors including wood species, size of piece, complexity, construction method, etc. Shoot me a message with what you have in mind and I'll be happy to discuss options and provide estimates.

Shipping/Delivery

Furniture: I provide free delivery within a couple hours drive of Seattle, Washington. Local pickup is always an option as well if you prefer. At the moment, I don't ship furniture outside the Seattle area. If you're not in my area, I encourage you to find a woodworker local to your area to work with to support your local economy and small business owners. If you aren't local, but really want a particular piece of mine, shoot me a message and we can discuss options.

Smaller Items: I can ship smaller items such as boxes and wall shelves within the United States. Shipping costs dependent on size and shipping method.
