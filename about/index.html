About

Understory woodworking is based in the Ballard neighborhood of Seattle, Washington. Understory focuses on making high quality custom furniture that will last a lifetime (or three). @todo add copy

* Old-timey map not intended for navigational purposes.

Quality

My goal in furniture making and woodworking is to build objects that remain functional and beautiful for a lifetime of use. This means I focus equally on the quality of materials and craftsmanship as well as aim for a timeless and universal aesthetic that won't [something] with fleeting trends. I hope that everythign I make will last as long as possible in order to stay in use and out of landfills.

Commission Work

Whether you have a vision of exactly what you're looking for or just an idea of the type of piece you want, I'm happy to work with you through the design process and to bring the piece to life. While I tend to focus on furniture making, I can also take on built-in cabinetry/shelving projects and the occasional smaller woodworking pieces.

All of the pieces found on this website are my own custom designs. I'm more than happy to build you any of the pieces you see here in a variety of different wood species or finishes. Given that the design work is already done for these pieces, turnaround will generally be a bit quicker than a project that includes new design work.

Sustainability

Sustainability is very important to me in every aspect of my life. And it's no different with my woodworking. From choosing sustainable domestic and local species of wood to minimizing waste, I do what I can to minimize my impact on the environment.
