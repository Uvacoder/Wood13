About

Understory woodworking is a one-person shop based in Seattle, Washington and run by B.J. Miller. My mission is to design and build high quality custom furniture that will last a lifetime (or three) and stay out of the landfill.

Commission Work

Whether you have a vision of exactly what you're looking for or just an idea of the type of piece you want, I'm happy to work with you through the design process and to bring the piece to life. While I tend to focus on furniture making, I can also take on built-in cabinetry/shelving projects and the occasional smaller woodworking pieces.

All of the pieces found on this website are my own custom designs. I'm more than happy to build you any of the pieces you see here in the wood species, dimensions, and finish of your choice. Given that the design work is already done for these pieces, turnaround will generally be a bit quicker than a project that includes new design work.

From time to time, I may also have pre-made pieces available for sale and immediate delivery. You can find these items listed on this site or via my Instagram feed.

Quality

In my woodworking, I aim to build objects that remain functional and beautiful for a lifetime of use. Starting with thoughtful design and quality materials, I employ traditional joinery and time-tested techniques to provide durability and timeless aesthetics. All of my work is designed with daily use and environmental conditions in mind.

Sustainability

From choosing sustainable domestic and local species of wood to minimizing waste, I do what I can to reduce my impact on the environment. In building high-quality and long-lasting furniture, my hope is to provide better and more sustainable alternatives to mass-produced, commercially available options.
